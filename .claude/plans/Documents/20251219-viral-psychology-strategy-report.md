## Executive Summary

Split Lease has **exceptional viral potential** because of three structural advantages:

1. **Inherently Multiplayer**: The split scheduling model *requires* multiple guests per apartment‚Äîvirality is baked into the business model
2. **High Emotional Stakes**: Housing is identity-defining; people talk about where they live

This report maps the 8 psychological motivation clusters that drive sharing behavior to specific Split Lease opportunities, ranked by implementation priority and expected impact.

## The "Broken Unless Shared" Principle

The research suggests making the product "broken unless people share." Split Lease has a **natural multiplayer mechanic**:

### Current State:
- Hosts benefit from multiple guests (higher occupancy)
- Guests benefit from split pricing (lower per-night cost)

### Enhancement Opportunities:

1. **Waitlist Priority**: "Refer 3 friends to skip the waitlist" (scarcity + sharing)

2. **Split Partner Matching**: "Find someone to share your schedule with" (literally broken without sharing)

3. **Referral-Unlocked Listings**: Some premium listings only available to referrers

4. **Host Earnings Multiplier**: "Your earnings increase 10% when you refer another host"

5. **Guest Savings Unlock**: "Refer 2 guests to unlock premium listings at standard rates"
